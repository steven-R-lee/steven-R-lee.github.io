what is the relationship between 'mindfulness' and 'self awareness' ?

When learning from experts such as Dan Goleman talking on Self Awareness and John Kabat-Zinn talking on Mindfulness, I recognise the relationship between the two practices as much the same thing: intertwined and hard to disentangle.

Self-awareness is about the actor being aware of his emotions by maintaining neutrality, whilst remaining self-reflective.
Mindfulness is about the purposely paying attention to a particular moment without judgement.

I chose to do Journaling because I have practiced the technique for more than a decade.
Historically, I used the journaling technique when working in mny high-pressured environments as a contractor. I practiced it almost everyday while sitting on the train on the way home. Relaxing in stillness and then writing positive and negative activites that I had experienced throughout my day.
I also extended my journaling technique by reviewing wha I had written on that weekday in the previous week. It always surprised me to discover how things I felt would be challenges turned out not to be, and the converse.

My experience today ... similar to those of yester-year.
The relaxed, almost semi-conscious flow of words onto the page acts as a therapy. Whereby, issues through words on a page come out which I was seemingly never fully conscious of prior to the activity. E.g. my fear of writing code again after so many years away from the scene.

I was surpised by what I wrote. It was revealing, and gives me something to work-on aand to overcome.
